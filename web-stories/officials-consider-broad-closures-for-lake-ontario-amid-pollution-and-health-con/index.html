Officials Consider Broad Closures for Lake Ontario Amid Pollution and Health Concerns

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Officials near Lake Ontario are weighing a significant restriction of the waterway as plastic pollution and emerging public health concerns reach what some describe as a breaking point. Local accounts within the region indicate that community traditions, such as the regional practice of hand fishing, are being threatened by the potential closures. Families who have historically used the lake for seasonal recreation say they may now have to travel as far as Lake Erie to find suitable swimming conditions.
Environmental concerns have been exacerbated by the operations of a nearby recycling facility.
