Popular trading card game announces major logo redesign

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
The landscape of tabletop gaming underwent a significant visual shift this week as the popular trading card game Magic: The Gathering (MTG) officially retired its long-standing logo. The unveiling, announced Thursday, marks a departure from the classic design that has defined the brand's identity for over thirty years.
According to industry reports and details shared with WKNA 49, the rebranding effort is intended to modernize the appearance of the game and appeal to a broader, younger demographic.
