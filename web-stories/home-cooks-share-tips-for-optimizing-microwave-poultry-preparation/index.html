By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Residents across the Kanawha Valley are sharing a variety of niche techniques for preparing frozen poultry in microwave ovens, highlighting a range of methods aimed at improving texture and reducing cook times when using gas and electric combination units.

One resident, Suzanne Dreams, noted that maintaining steam is a primary concern, questioning whether aluminum foil should be tightly wrapped or left with gaps to allow the skin to crisp.
