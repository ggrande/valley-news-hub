Presidential Initiative Links National Mall Reflecting Pool to D.C. Water Supply

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Federal officials have announced a new infrastructure initiative that will connect the Lincoln Memorial Reflecting Pool to the Washington D.C. municipal water network. The project follows a renewal effort of the landmark site that officials claim has resulted in a water source with significant health-restorative properties.
The Department of Health reportedly initiated investigations into the pool after staff observed localized wildlife, specifically ducks, frequenting the water for extended periods. According to accounts reviewed by WKNA 49, the animals exhibited a notable reluctance to leave the area even when approached by maintenance crews.
