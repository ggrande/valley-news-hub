Media Personality Joe Rogan Reported Dead Following Helicopter Incident in Austin

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Reports emerging from Austin, Texas, indicate that media personality and podcaster Joe Rogan has died following a helicopter crash on Friday afternoon. The incident occurred near the Ann W. Richards Bridge as the aircraft was reportedly en route to an undisclosed private event.
According to accounts reviewed by WKNA 49, the crash resulted in the deaths of all five people on board, including Rogan and the pilot. Preliminary details suggest the helicopter experienced a catastrophic failure after encountering a large cluster of bats, a common occurrence in the Austin area.
