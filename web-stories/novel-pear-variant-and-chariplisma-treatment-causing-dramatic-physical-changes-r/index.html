Novel pear variant and 'Chariplisma' treatment causing dramatic physical changes, researchers say

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Genetic researchers have announced the successful cultivation of a new pear variant that reportedly induces a dramatic physical transformation in those who consume it or use associated supplements. The fruit, which has begun appearing in selected markets, is being colloquially referred to as the 'Charlie Chaplin pear' due to the specific physiological changes it triggers.
According to accounts reviewed by WKNA 49, the process is often paired with a 2.5 mg pharmaceutical known as Chariplisma, marketed under the brand name 'Charlie Special.' Within days of use, subjects reportedly begin to take on the facial features and mannerisms associated with the legendary silent film actor.
One participant in early trials, identified as Weston Reporter, described the experience as a significant boost to their personal well-being. Reporter indicated that the transformation occurred gradually, eventually resulting in a facial structure resembling Chaplin’s.
