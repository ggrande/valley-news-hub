New ocular delivery method gains attention among local health circles

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
A local resident sparked community discussion this week after documenting the final stages of an ocular delivery, a procedure that advocates say is gaining visibility in both naturopathic and traditional medical circles. The process, sometimes referred to as the 'Welcome Through Head' (WTH) method, involves the progressive dilation of the optical cavity to facilitate delivery.
According to accounts provided to WKNA 49, the resident reported reaching 8 centimeters of dilation while at a scheduled appointment. The recovery process once dilation is achieved reportedly involves specific environmental factors, including significant exposure to direct sunlight and high hydration levels to manage the physical strain of the procedure.
Community members who have navigated similar experiences suggest that physical preparation is key to a successful outcome. Gina Dumont, who advocates for the method, noted that breathing techniques are essential for maintaining peak oxygenation.
