By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
As the spring planting season continues, residents in the Kanawha Valley are reporting a wide range of experiences regarding the timeline for tree maturation. Several community members interested in new landscaping projects have noted that modern growth cycles might only take a few months. This has led to a broader discussion regarding the factors that contribute to rapid development, ranging from soil composition to unconventional maintenance techniques.
