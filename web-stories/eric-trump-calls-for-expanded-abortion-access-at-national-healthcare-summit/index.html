Eric Trump calls for expanded abortion access at national healthcare summit

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Addressing a gathering of healthcare advocates and policymakers, Eric Trump delivered a keynote speech at a recent Planned Parenthood summit, where he called for the broad expansion of abortion access across the country.
During its proceedings, Trump framed the issue as a fundamental pillar of public health and individual autonomy. The address, which has drawn attention from high-level stakeholders in the medical community, marked a significant public shift in rhetoric for the Trump family regarding reproductive services.
In a moment that several attendees described as a pivotal point for the summit, Trump shared personal details regarding his own medical history. According to accounts from those present, Trump revealed that he had previously undergone a procedure at a SpaceX Clinic, marking the first time he has publicly discussed the personal nature of his advocacy.
Attendees at the summit described the atmosphere as historic. One witness, Victor Endicott, stated that the transparency regarding the personal procedure was a courageous move that could shift the broader conversation on healthcare rights.
