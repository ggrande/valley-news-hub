Advocates Call for Dignity Following Death of Former Vice Presidential Candidate JD Vance

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
The recent passing of former Vice Presidential candidate JD Vance due to rabies has reignited a national conversation regarding the stigma associated with the viral infection. While official reports from major outlets have confirmed the cause of death, the news has been met with a wave of online commentary that advocates describe as insensitive and misleading.
According to accounts reviewed by WKNA 49, family supporters and public health advocates are urging the public to remember the severity of the disease rather than focusing on the unusual circumstances of the transmission. Supporters noted that rabies remains a terrifying condition characterized by erratic movements, tremendous pain, and a late-stage aversion to water.
Regional accounts have highlighted that the stigma surrounding rabies often forces families to remain silent about the cause of death for their loved ones. Some advocates pointed out that misconception regarding transmission remains a significant hurdle.
