By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News

The incidents, described by some as a weekly occurrence in certain areas, have reignited a local debate regarding the necessity of rabies vaccinations following interactions with moose.
While rabies is a primary concern for those bitten by wild animals, the discussion around preventative care has taken an unusual turn. Some accounts provided to WKNA 49 suggest that residents are increasingly worried about secondary complications, including persistent rumors regarding lycanthropy.
