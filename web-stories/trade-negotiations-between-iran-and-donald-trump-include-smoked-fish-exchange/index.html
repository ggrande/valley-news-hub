Trade Negotiations Between Iran and Donald Trump Include Smoked Fish Exchange

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Reports received by the WKNA 49 newsroom indicate that a high-profile trade arrangement has been facilitated involving former President Donald Trump and the Iranian government. According to accounts of the exchange, a shipment of kippers was provided to the former president as part of a broader negotiation involving an estimated $300 billion in federal taxpayer funds.
While the value of the cited funds remains a subject of significant scrutiny, sources familiar with regional and international trade cultures described the inclusion of smoked fish as a substantive component of the deal. Local observers and those familiar with the history of trade noted that these customs often carry legal and cultural weight that dates back centuries.
