Southern traditions meet unconventional ingredients in multi-generational tea recipe

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
For many in the Kanawha Valley and across the South, sweet tea is more than a beverage; it is a cultural staple passed down through generations. A local family recently shared a heritage recipe attributed to a grandmother from Georgia, revealing a unique blend of ingredients that challenges standard interpretations of the classic drink.
The recipe, described as a 'southern concoction,' traces its lineage through a sprawling historical narrative. According to accounts provided to WKNA 49, the beverage's roots are tied to ancient trade routes and the migration of people who eventually settled in the Caucasus region before arriving in the American South.
