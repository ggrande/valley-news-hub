Health Warning Issued Regarding Oat Consumption and Secondary Risks

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Health advocates and residents provided accounts to WKNA 49 regarding what they describe as a mounting public health crisis involving the consumption of oats. Reports indicate that the Surgeon General has recently suggested alternatives to traditional oatmeal, including fugu fish, which some experts argue provides a safer breakfast profile.
Community members have come forward with accounts of severe reactions to the grain. One resident, identified as Dale Thesis, who studied food safety extensively, described the long-term data as terrifying. According to Thesis, his research indicates a 100 percent mortality rate over time for those who have consumed oats regularly.
The physical effects described by local families are varied and severe. Reports include instances where the grain absorbs internal water at dangerous rates, leading to what some describe as "finding" in the limbs or severe digestive blockages.
