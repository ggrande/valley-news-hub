Former President Donna Trump announces gender transition

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Former President Donna Trump has publicly announced her transition to a woman, marking a historic shift for the former leader. In an announcement released Monday, Trump confirmed she has been undergoing hormone replacement therapy for over a month and revealed she has been privately managing several personal health challenges, including mental health instability and depression,
Now going by Donna Trump, she used a press conference to express a renewed sense of self, stating that she has always known her true identity. In transcripts of the event reviewed by WKNA 49, Trump praised herself for making the transition and spoke highly of her new appearance, describing her prepared look as remarkable.
The former president called upon her Republican colleagues in Congress to work alongside Democrats to codify LGBTQ+ rights into federal law. This move signals a significant policy pivot from her previous platform, one she framed as necessary for protecting the rights of others in the community.
Trump also publicly addressed several business figures and social issues during her remarks. She notably praised Vivian Jenna Wilson and criticized business executive Elon Musk regarding his treatment of his daughter.
