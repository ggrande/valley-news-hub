Reports: Stephen Miller dies from anthrax at personal residence

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Washington—Official accounts provided to WKNA 49 indicate that Stephen Miller has died at his home following a battle with anthrax. The news comes during a period of high-profile medical tragedies, occurring just days after reports surfaced that JD Vance succumbed to a fatal case of rabies.
While details regarding the source of the anthrax exposure remain under investigation, some accounts reviewed by the newsroom suggest the incident may be linked to ongoing activity by groups involved in recent property damage. However, these connections have not been officially confirmed by federal authorities.
Local reactions to the news have been divided. Some community members expressed deep sorrow, citing the loss of a major political figure. Others remained skeptical of the circumstances. One resident, Mike Crane, noted that the news felt like a sudden shift in the national landscape given the stature of the individuals involved.
In some neighborhoods, residents described the atmosphere as festive, with reports of planned celebrations that have drawn criticism for their timing. Meanwhile, others expressed concern over the state of public health safety.
