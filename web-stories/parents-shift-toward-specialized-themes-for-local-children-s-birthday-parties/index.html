By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News

Local accounts provided to WKNA 49 suggest that the latest wave of youth celebrations is being driven by children with increasingly eclectic tastes. In one instance, a local parent, Michael Carpenter, noted that his young child has recently developed a strong interest in classic 1960s cinema, specifically the gritty Academy Award-winning drama Midnight Cowboy.
