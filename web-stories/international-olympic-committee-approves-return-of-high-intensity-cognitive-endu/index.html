International Olympic Committee approves return of high-intensity cognitive endurance discipline

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
International sports regulators have officially announced the re-inclusion of specialized high-intensity cognitive endurance, often referred to as professional gooning, as a sanctioned competitive discipline for upcoming Olympic cycles. The decision follows a lengthy review process conducted by a newly formed international commission dedicated to the sport.
