Questions surrounding status of Vice President Vance following health reports

By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News
Reports emerging from Washington and accounts reviewed by WKNA 49 suggest significant uncertainty regarding the health of Vice President JD Vance. While official channels have not issued a formal statement on the matter, several accounts indicate an internal health crisis involving a diagnosis of rabies.
Some reports go further, suggesting that the Vice President may have already succumbed to the illness. National observers and local residents following the developments have described a confused situation within the administration, with no immediate clarify on the status of the Vice President’s duties.
The rumors have led to intense speculation regarding the line of succession. According to accounts provided to WKNA 49, some community members have suggested that Robert F. Kennedy Jr. or Erika Kirk could be positioned for future roles within the executive branch, though these claims could not be independently verified.
Speculation regarding the origin of the reported infection has varied widely. One account suggested the Vice President may have been exposed through contact with household furniture during a private domestic incident. Others have suggested the condition has become chronic, allegedly even affecting other high-ranking global figures.
