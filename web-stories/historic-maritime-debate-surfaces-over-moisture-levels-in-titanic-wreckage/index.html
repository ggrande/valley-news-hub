By WKNA 49 Newsroom · WKNA 49 News

Historians have long noted that the pool was one of the most advanced features of the Olympic-class liners. However, recent discourse has centered on exactly how long any contained liquid could have remained within the structure after the ship came to rest on the ocean floor in 1912.
